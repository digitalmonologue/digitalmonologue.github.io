Why Sharing History is Important

了解家族病史的重要性：给长辈的核心要点

1. Find problems early (及早发现问题)

Knowing your history helps doctors decide when to start checks. If relatives had colorectal cancer, you might start at 40 instead of 50.

了解病史有助于医生决定何时开始检查。例如，如果亲属患有肠癌，筛查可能需要从40岁开始，而不是50岁。

2. Save your life (挽救生命)

Diseases like cancer are much more treatable when caught early. Early detection is the first step to staying healthy for us.

癌症等疾病在早期发现时更容易治疗。及早发现是保持健康的第一步。

3. Not just cancer (不只是癌症)

Family history is also a key factor for other common conditions, such as heart disease, stroke, and diabetes.

家族史不仅与癌症有关，也是评估心脏病、中风和糖尿病风险的关键因素。

4. Take action (积极采取行动)

Once known, you can work with your doctor on a plan including regular check-ups and maintaining healthy habits.

了解病史后，您可以与医生共同制定计划，包括定期检查和保持良好的生活习惯。
