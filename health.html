Why Family Medical History is Important

为什么分享家族病史对长辈至关重要

1. It helps find problems early (早发现、早治疗)

Knowing your family history helps doctors decide when you should start getting checked. For example, if parents or siblings had colorectal cancer, you may need to start screening at 40 instead of 50. If a mother or sister had breast cancer, you are at higher risk and should get mammograms more frequently.

2. It can save your life (挽救生命)

Many articles emphasize that diseases like cancer are much more treatable when caught early. For instance, the 5-year survival rate for early-stage colorectal cancer is over 90%. Knowing your risk is the first step to early detection.

3. It's not just about cancer (不只是癌症)

Family history is also a key factor for other common conditions, such as heart disease and stroke.

4. You can take action (积极采取行动)

Once you know your family history, you can work with your doctor to make a plan, including regular check-ups (taking advantage of free screening programs) and healthy habits (eating well, exercising, avoiding smoking).
