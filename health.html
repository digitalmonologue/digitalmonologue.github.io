Why Sharing History is Important

了解家族病史的重要性：给长辈的核心要点

1. Find problems early (及早发现问题)

Knowing your history helps doctors decide when to start checks. If relatives had colorectal cancer, you might start at 40 instead of 50.

了解病史有助于医生决定何时开始检查。例如，如果亲属患有肠癌，筛查可能需要从40岁开始，而不是50岁。

2. Save your life (挽救生命)

Diseases like cancer are much more treatable when caught early. Early detection is the first step to staying healthy for us.

癌症等疾病在早期发现时更容易治疗。及早发现是保持健康的第一步。

3. Not just cancer (不只是癌症)

Family history is also a key factor for other common conditions, such as heart disease, stroke, and diabetes.

家族史不仅与癌症有关，也是评估心脏病、中风和糖尿病风险的关键因素。

4. Take action (积极采取行动)

Once known, you can work with your doctor on a plan including regular check-ups and maintaining healthy habits.

了解病史后，您可以与医生共同制定计划，包括定期检查和保持良好的生活习惯。

Full Investigative Checklist / 详细家族疾病查询清单

Use this list as a reference when interviewing relatives about the categories above.
在询问亲属时，请参考此清单了解上述各类别的具体疾病内容。

1. Cardiovascular & Blood / 心血管与血液
  • Coronary Artery Disease / 冠心病 (Inc. Heart Attack)
  • Hypertension / 高血压
  • Hyperlipidemia / 高血脂 (Cholesterol/Triglycerides)
  • Stroke / 中风 (Ischemic or Hemorrhagic)
  • Aneurysms / 动脉瘤 (Abdominal or Cerebral)
  • Blood Disorders / 血液病 (Anemia/Hemophilia)
  • Deep Vein Thrombosis (DVT) / 深静脉血栓
2. Cancer History / 癌症病史
  • Breast & Ovarian / 乳腺癌及卵巢癌 (Esp. before age 50)
  • Colorectal / 结直肠癌 (Inc. Polyps)
  • Prostate Cancer / 前列腺癌
  • Pancreatic Cancer / 胰腺癌
  • Melanoma / 黑色素瘤 (or recurring skin cancers)
  • Leukemia or Lymphoma / 白血病或淋巴瘤
3. Endocrine & Metabolic / 内分泌与代谢
  • Diabetes / 糖尿病 (Type 1, 2, or Gestational)
  • Thyroid Disease / 甲状腺疾病 (Goiters/Hyper/Hypo)
  • PCOS / 多囊卵巢综合征
  • Obesity / 肥胖症 (Metabolic Syndrome)
4. Neuro & Mental Health / 神经与心理
  • Dementia / 痴呆症 (Alzheimer's or Vascular)
  • Movement Disorders / 运动障碍 (Parkinson's)
  • Seizure Disorders / 癫痫
  • Mood Disorders / 情绪障碍 (Depression/Anxiety)
  • Psychotic Disorders / 精神分裂症
  • Addiction / 成瘾 (Alcoholism)
5. Autoimmune & Inflamm. / 自身免疫与炎症
  • Arthritis / 关节炎 (Gout/Rheumatoid/Osteo)
  • Digestive Issues / 消化系统 (Crohn's/Colitis)
  • Skin Conditions / 皮肤病 (Eczema/Psoriasis)
  • Lupus (SLE) / 红斑狼疮
  • Multiple Sclerosis (MS) / 多发性硬化症
6. Respiratory & Allergy / 呼吸与过敏
  • Asthma / 哮喘 (Childhood or Adult-onset)
  • COPD / 慢性阻塞性肺病 (Emphysema/Bronchitis)
  • Severe Allergies / 严重过敏 (Food/Medication)
  • Cystic Fibrosis / 囊性纤维化
7. Sensory & Organ Health / 感官与器官
  • Vision Loss / 视力丧失 (Glaucoma/Macular/Cataracts)
  • Hearing Loss / 听力丧失 (Early-onset)
  • Kidney Disease / 肾脏疾病 (PKD/Failure)
  • Liver Disease / 肝脏疾病 (Cirrhosis/Iron overload)
8. Pregnancy & Development / 怀孕与发育
  • Infertility / 不孕不育 (History of IVF)
  • Miscarriages / 反复流产 (3 or more)
  • Birth Defects / 出生缺陷 (Heart/Cleft Palate)
  • Developmental Delay / 发育迟缓 (Autism/Downs)
