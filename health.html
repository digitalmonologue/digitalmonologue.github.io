For the Doctor (English)

请把这段话给医生看，或直接读给他们听

"I haven't had a check-up in a long time. My son is a Data Scientist and is helping me organize our family medical history. Since I am of Chinese descent and over 50, I would like to request the following screenings covered by Medicare:"

“我很久没做身体检查了。我的儿子是一名数据科学家，他正在帮我整理家族病史。考虑到我是华裔且年龄超过50岁，我希望进行以下受 Medicare 资助的检查：”

  • Heart Health Check (MBS Item 699)

    心脏健康检查 - 检查血压、胆固醇水平及心血管风险。

  • Diabetes Screening (HbA1c Test)

    糖尿病筛查 - 检查血糖水平。

  • Mammogram & Cervical Screening

    乳腺癌及宫颈癌筛查。

  • National Bowel Cancer Screening

    肠癌筛查 - 确认是否需要新的测试包。

Checklist 就诊准备

  • Medicare Card (Green) / Medicare卡
  • Current Medications / 正在服用的药物
  • Fasting? (Ask GP) / 是否需要空腹？
